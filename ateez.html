XG is a seven-member Hip-hop/R&B-inspired girl group comprising JURIN, CHISA, HINATA, HARVEY, JURIA, MAYA, and COCONA. XG debuted with the single "Tippy Toes" in March 2022 under the global entertainment production company " XGALX," renowned for fostering artist groups with distinctive worldviews and a bold cultural impact, resonating globally. They've achieved groundbreaking milestones worldwide, including securing the top spot on the US Billboard chart "Hot Trending Songs Powered by Twitter" the first Japanese artists to do so. They also garced the cover of esteemed US magazine "Billboard", again the first Japanese girl group to achieve this. The name "XG," short for "Xtraordinary Girls," embodies their mission to empower individuals from diverse backgrounds worldwide conventional through their unconventional musical styles and performances, transcending norms and standards.
